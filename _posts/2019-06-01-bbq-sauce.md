title: BBQ Sauce
ingredients:
  - 2 cups ketchup
  - 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  - 1/4 cup brown sugar
  - 2 tablespoons honey
  - 1 tablespoon coconut aminos
  - 1 teaspoon salt
  - 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  - 1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
  - 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  - 1/8 teaspoon cumin
instructions:
  - Mix all ingredients until well combined
date: 2019-06-01 21:25:00 -0700
---
